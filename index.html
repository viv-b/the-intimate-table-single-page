The Intimate Table

Beautiful food relies on the basic premise that it is the food of the moment. As Nigel Slater states, the best recipe of all is “right food, right place right time…”, a cold nectarine by the sea in summer; hair dripping with salt, a leftover chicken and stuffing sandwich 26/12, blue mornings of buttered toast and velvet bitter coffee or a dark chocolate-covered ginger biscuit kissed by jasmine tea on a frost-bitten night in June.

Teaming up with creative brands to deliver delicious, seasonal food and beverage. Menus, recipes, food styling and content creation. Welcome to The Intimate Table.
